# Privacy Policy

!!! notice
    By using davidnet, you agree to this privacy policy.
    
    If you do not agree, please stop using davidnet and delete your data at [My Account](https://account.davidnet.net/account/), if you have an account.

---

## In Short
- We process data inside the EU.
- We do **not** collect marketing information.
- We do **not** sell your information.
- We do **not** use analytics.
- You may **delete** your data at [My Account](https://account.davidnet.net/account/).

## 1. What Data Do We Collect?
- **User-Agent**: Session information.  
- **IP Address**: Security, maintenance, and session information. 
- **Email**: Contact and account recovery.  
- **User Content**: Images or other data you provide to us through uploading content.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
We only collect data necessary for davidnet to function properly, including providing and maintaining our services.

## 3. Sharing Your Data
Most of Davidnet is self-hosted. However, for some small parts, we use third-party services. We may share your personal data with third-party service providers, such as [Strato](https://www.strato.com/), who assist us in hosting our services. 

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Cookies & Local Storage
We use cookies and local storage that are **essential** for davidnet to work.  
These cookies and local storage **cannot be turned off** and are **not used** for marketing or additional data collection.

## 5. Data Retention
We will retain your data until you delete it. Logs will be deleted after a service is removed or updated, typically within less than one month.

## 6. Security Measures
For information on our security practices, please visit our [Security](https://docs.davidnet.net/legal/security) page.

## 7. Data Deletion
You may delete your data at [My Account](https://account.davidnet.net/account/).

## 8. Contact Us
For any questions or feedback, please email us at [contact@davidnet.net](mailto:contact@davidnet.net).
